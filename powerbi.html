Power BI

Road Accident project:

In this Power BI project, I addressed the client's need for a comprehensive Road Accident Dashboard for the years 2021 and 2022. Leveraging Power BI functionalities, I focused on key performance indicators (KPIs) such as total casualties and accidents, providing both current year insights and year-over-year growth. Through effective data modeling, I established relationships between multiple tables, enabling seamless analysis across various dimensions. Utilizing Data Analysis Expressions (DAX), I calculated year-to-date and year-over-year growth metrics.
